<Shut Down>


2024

Narrative

Producer, Director, D.P., Editor


On the brink of ending his life, Chung receives an unexpected delivery. Inside the package is a cryptic card that reads: “Interview Terminated.” Awakening in a sterile white space, he realizes he’s part of a high-stakes simulation — a job interview that manipulates his perception and memories. When the session ends, strange events begin haunting his real world. As reality collapses into illusion, Chung must confront whether he’s truly free — or still inside someone else’s test.
